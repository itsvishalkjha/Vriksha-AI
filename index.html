Frequently Asked Questions

What is vriksha.ai?

vriksha.ai is a specialized B2B solutions provider. The word "Vriksha" means tree in Sanskrit, symbolizing our core architecture: deep roots in data protection, a strong trunk of legal compliance, and branching AI processes that deliver custom enterprise automations.

How does Climate ESG Compliance AI work?

Our ESG systems monitor raw telemetry, ERP databases, and supply chain invoices. The engine matches this data with active frameworks like CSRD, GRI, and SASB, automatically predicting regulatory risks and compiling audit-ready compliance dossiers.

Can we deploy vriksha.ai on our own cloud infrastructure?

Yes. Absolute compliance is our priority. vriksha.ai offers hybrid cloud integrations and private VPC deployments via AWS, GCP, or Azure, guaranteeing that zero operational data leaves your company bounds.
